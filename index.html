Our Doctrines

  • The Bible is the inspired Word of God.
  • Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.
  • The Trinity: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • Jesus Christ is fully God and fully man.
  • The Church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, and serve.
  • Believer’s baptism and the Lord’s Supper are ordinances of the church.
  • We await the return of Jesus Christ.
